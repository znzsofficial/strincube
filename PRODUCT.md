# Product

## Register

product

## Users

StrinCube is primarily a personal play and world-editing tool for the project owner and a small circle of players. Users are usually creating, testing, saving, and revisiting block worlds rather than browsing a public marketing site.

## Product Purpose

The product provides a cute block-world sandbox with procedural terrain, infinite chunk loading, building, TNT interactions, water, creatures, imported models, and persistent saves. Success means users can quickly create a world, understand the generation choices, enter play, and trust that their edits persist.

## Brand Personality

Cute, playful, and practical. The interface should feel game-like and friendly without hiding controls or making repeated setup work slow.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS dashboards, dense developer-only configuration forms, oversized marketing heroes, and decorative UI that makes game setup harder to scan. Avoid making the creation flow feel like a serious admin panel.

## Design Principles

Make world creation legible at a glance.
Prefer playful controls when they clarify choices.
Keep creation and debugging workflows fast.
Show only settings that matter for the selected world mode.
Protect trust around saving, loading, and generated worlds.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain basic readability across desktop and mobile: clear labels, adequate contrast, comfortable touch targets, visible focus states, and layouts that do not rely on color alone.
